Introduction

Experience the future of interactive entertainment with our 3D LED Holographic Display. This innovative device goes beyond traditional displays, offering users the ability to showcase 3D Objects, Engaging Animations, and Simple Games. Users can join our collaborative platform and connect, share their creativity, and get inspired by others. Users hold the reins, deciding how to wield the cube’s versatile display.

Standout Features

  • Effortlessly showcase your chosen 3D object files, transforming them into captivating visual experiences.
  • Bring your content to life with the ability to play simple animations.
  • Immerse yourself in inbuilt interactive games for a dynamic and engaging experience.
  • Enjoy seamless touch control for intuitive navigation.
  • Stay connected globally—our display is cloud-enabled, allowing you to control it from anywhere in the world.

Testing

In the testing strategy for our 3D LED Cube Holographic Display project, a multi-tiered approach will be employed to ensure the thorough examination of both software and hardware components. Unit testing will be implemented to scrutinize isolated functions, specifically focusing on tasks such as mapping 3D object files and rendering various 3D graphs. This level of testing aims to verify the correctness and reliability of individual functions within the software controlling the LED Cube. Moving to widget or component testing, the emphasis will shift to the interaction and behavior of individual UI components, such as buttons or sliders. This will ensure the seamless integration of external controls with the LED Cube, validating their responsiveness and accuracy. Lastly, integrated testing will provide a holistic evaluation of the entire LED Cube system. This involves simulating real-world scenarios, such as connecting the LED Cube to a computer and running the software to display 3D objects, ensuring the cohesive functionality of both hardware and software components. Through this comprehensive testing plan, we aim to guarantee the robustness and reliability of the our project across various levels of functionality and user experience.

Creating the LED Cube

The 8X8X16 LED tower was created by our project team by grounding the LEDs to diffuse them and then soldering them layer by layer.
